How I Got Here

Without any intention of becoming a programmer or software developer, I joined Moringa as a means of finding a good and productive way to occupy my idle time. Since joining I have developed somewhat of an interest in the field and am excited to see where the journey takes me. I especially enjoy the google meets sessions and the combination of respectful students and witty, engaging TM's(Shoutout George and Edgar).

Things I Like

In this wonderful life there are a lot of things to like. I personally like when it rains, a lot, especially if the rain is accompanied by bright streaks of lightning and deafning roars of thunder. There's just something about the rain that reminds you of how powerless you are against the great force of mother nature. I like being trapped inside when it rains and having an excuse to wear cozy clothes and throw away the day, a day that would otherwise probably be as hot as the pits of hell itself. I also love music and anything guitar related. Music never lets you down.

Things I Don't Like

I don't quite like the feeling of knowing what you want but not knowing how you can get to it. I don't really like the number 1( it's just a little odd). I don't like how funny I think this is. I don't like when it doesn't rain.
